Privacy Policy for WOMP

Last Updated: May 29, 2025

Thank you for using WOMP (the "Application"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Application and the choices you have associated with that data.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data through the Application.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect any personally identifiable information from its users. You can use the Application anonymously without providing any personal data.

"runFullTrust" Permission

Our Application is submitted to the Microsoft Store and may declare the "runFullTrust" capability. This capability is a standard permission for many desktop applications to function correctly on Windows, allowing the application to operate with the user's full permissions on their device.

Despite this permission, WOMP does not use this capability to access, collect, store, or transmit any of your personal files or data. The Application's functionality is self-contained and does not involve the collection or processing of user data.

Log Data

The Application does not generate or store any log data that includes personal information.

Cookies

The Application does not use "cookies" or similar tracking technologies.

Service Providers

As no data is collected, no data is shared with any service providers.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

Our Application does not address anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information at all, we do not collect it from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
